This document is about odometry calculations

Currently Calculates:
* New bot position in local coordinates
* Bot rotation in local coordinates
Variables
* LeftRotations - the rotational value for the left odometry tracker
* RightRotations - the rotational value for the right odometry tracker
* LeftSpacing - distance from wheel center to origin, assuming wheel is horizontal to origin
* RightSpacing - distance from wheel center to origin, assuming wheel is horizontal to origin
* LeftDiameter - left wheel diameter
* RightDiameter - right wheel diameter
* TravelLeft - travel of left wheel
* TravelRight - travel of right wheel
* RadiusLeft - radius of arc made by left wheel
* RadiusRight - radius of arc made by right wheel
